║                    PROCESSING CHAIN RATIONALE                                ║
╠══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╣
║                                                                              ║
║  QUESTION: Why per-segment loudnorm BEFORE stitching?                       ║
║                                                                              ║
║  ANSWER:                                                                    ║
║    1. Each source has different recording levels                            ║
║    2. Normalizing per-segment ensures equal energy before crossfade         ║
║    3. Prevents "jumping" loudness between segments                          ║
║    4. Crossfades blend more smoothly with matched levels                    ║
║                                                                              ║
║  QUESTION: Why noise reduction AFTER stitching?                             ║
║                                                                              ║
║  ANSWER:                                                                    ║
║    1. Stitched audio has consistent noise floor (from same processing)     ║
║    2. Single noise reduction pass more efficient than per-segment          ║
║    3. Avoids processing artifacts at segment boundaries                     ║
║    4. GENTLE settings preserve voice quality                                ║
║                                                                              ║
║  QUESTION: Why final loudnorm pass?                                         ║
║                                                                              ║
║  ANSWER:                                                                    ║
║    1. Noise reduction may change loudness slightly                         ║
║    2. Ensures exact -16 LUFS target                                         ║
║    3. Guarantees broadcast-ready output                                     ║
║                                                                              ║
